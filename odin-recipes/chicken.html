Chicken Stir-Fry

Description

This chicken stir-fry recipe is packed with veggies and is quick and easy to prepare. Try adding bean sprouts, bamboo shoots, snap peas, or any of your favorite vegetables. Serve it with white or brown rice, or noodles.

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Corn starch
  • Minced garlic
  • Minced fresh ginger
  • Red pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water and rice
  2. Combine soy sauce and sugar with cornstarc
  3. Add sezane oil in wok and fry vegetables
  4. Serve over the rice
